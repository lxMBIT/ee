Antony Waldhorn

Standing out among his fellow producers with his signature orchestral sound, 24-year-old Anton Morozov (better known as Antony Waldhorn) is already an established name in the global trance scene.

"Lot of musicians are trying to provide to the world Quality music. First of all You should know: Music has no quality concept. It’s a resonance of our emotions and feelings, caused by all around us and expressed in the form of sound."
— Antony Waldhorn

Biotones

Biotones (as known as Deepshader) born in Republic of Moldova - it's a project basically oriented on producing atmospheric and groovy progressive house and trance, as well as deep / ambient / sountrack music.

One of the permanent headliners of the Moldavian Trance Scene, played on the same stage with Richard Durand, Stoneface & Terminal, Super8 & Tab, Jenya Solid, Andrew Rayel, Mat Zo, Leon Bolier etc.
